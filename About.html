About Warner Bros

Founded in 1923, Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc. is a global leader in the creation, production, distribution, licensing, and marketing of all forms of entertainment and their related businesses. Our vast library, one of the most prestigious and valuable in the world, consists of more than 100,000 hours of programming, including nearly 8,600 feature films and 5,000 television programs comprised of tens of thousands of individual episodes.

Warner Bros. continues to be at the forefront of entertainment and technology, with an innovative approach that combines our historical heritage with cutting-edge advances. From beloved classics to the latest blockbusters, Warner Bros. delivers a diverse range of entertainment to audiences around the world.

Our mission is to bring stories and experiences to life, and to deliver world-class content that engages, entertains, and inspires audiences everywhere.
